Basal Body Temperature

Track your waking temperature every morning. The thermal shift after ovulation is your most reliable confirmation of fertility status. Supports both °F and °C.

Simple to start

Three steps to understanding your cycle

1
Log daily observations

Spend 2 minutes each morning entering your waking temperature and daily fertility signs. The app guides you through each observation type.

2
Watch patterns emerge

After a few cycles, your unique biphasic pattern becomes clear. The interactive chart reveals your fertile window, ovulation day, and cycle length.

3
Know your body deeply

Use evidence-based FAM knowledge to understand your health, plan or avoid pregnancy naturally, and detect cycle irregularities early.

Your data is yours

Privacy isn't a feature.

In a world where reproductive health data is increasingly sensitive, CycleMonitor was built from day one to keep your most personal information private.

Self-host on your own server, or use our hosted version — either way, we never sell, share, or analyze your cycle data. Your body's rhythms belong to you.
