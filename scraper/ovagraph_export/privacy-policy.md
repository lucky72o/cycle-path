## Privacy Policy

### Ovagraph User Accounts

If you choose to create an account at Ovagraph.com or through the mobile application to track your fertility or participate in our forums, the personal data that you choose to provide us may be used in the following ways:

- To help in predicting your ovulation.
- To build charts and calendars of your personal data.
- Your forum posts and responses generated will be visible to other users.

Any personal data entered and saved to your OvaGraph account will not be shared, used or sold to any third party groups or organizations.

It is our goal to respect the privacy concerns and protect the data of all our OvaGraph app and website users. All information entered to your account on OvaGraph is uploaded to our secure server, and we have certain features in place to prevent unauthorized access to our user accounts, data and personal information. None of the information uploaded on our secure servers is shared, tracked, sold or accessible to third parties, vendors, or affiliates. The services provided to you through OvaGraph will also never include any advertisements or links to third party websites. Any links, alerts, or promotional material on OvaGraph are solely owned and controlled by Fairhaven Health.

If you wish to further secure the visibility of your account, please follow the instructions below.

### Visibility of Your Personal Data

You can choose who can see your personal data, charts, calendars, and cycle information by adjusting your sharing preferences in your User Chart Settings .

You can also manage your forum activity and email preferences from your account by clicking on the My Account section of the website.

### Cancelling your Account

You may cancel your account in your My Account settings at any time.  If you have questions or concerns regarding your personal data after cancelling your account, please reach out to us at info@ovagraph.com .

### Account History

Personal data you provide will be stored for the duration of your account.  We also store various logs to help us improve and troubleshoot the operation of Ovagraph.com.  Communication logs are purged every 7 days and general server logs are purged every 21 days.  Logs are not publicly accessible.

OvaGraph operates under the Fairhaven Health family of products, websites, and mobile applications.  The Fairhaven Health privacy policy can be found here .
