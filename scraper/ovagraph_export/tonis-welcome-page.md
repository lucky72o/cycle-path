## Toni's Welcome Page

“Fertility charting provides essential insight into your menstrual cycle, giving you the practical knowledge you need to take control of your reproductive health and fertility.  If you’ve read my book, Taking Charge of Your Fertility , you know that all you really need to chart your fertility is a BBT thermometer and a chart. While that remains true, many women find digital fertility charting to be more convenient, and enjoy the sophistication that technology adds to this fundamental life skill. So, whether you are actively trying to conceive or simply want to understand your menstrual cycle better, I know you will find everything you need here at OvaGraph to store and track your fertility, and a warm, safe community in which to ask questions and share your journey.” - Toni Weschler, author of Taking Charge of Your Fertility
